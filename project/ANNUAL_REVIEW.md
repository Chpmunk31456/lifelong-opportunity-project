# Annual Factual Review

Review every guide by July 2027 or sooner after a material change.

## Review Areas

- Wages and employment outlook
- Licensing and credential requirements
- Accreditation and program eligibility
- Apprenticeship and employer-supported education
- Education benefits, tax rules, and repayment terms
- Worker protections and safety
- Technology and ethical AI guidance
- Accessibility
- Official links and references
- Translation accuracy and localization

## Per-Guide Record

Record:

- Guide number, title, language, and version
- Review date and reviewer
- Sources checked and their access dates
- Material changes found
- Corrections made
- Accessibility review status
- Human translation-review status
- Next review date

Never treat a successful link check as a complete factual review.
