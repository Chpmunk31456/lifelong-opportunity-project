## Project Mission

This project helps people investigate realistic education and career paths using plain language, accessibility, ethical AI guidance, free-first learning options, employer-supported education, and clear warnings against guarantees or predatory programs.

The guides are designed for students, career changers, older adults, people with disabilities, workers seeking advancement, families planning education, and community organizations helping people find opportunity.

## Core Commitments

- No employment, income, admission, funding, licensing, or promotion guarantees
- Free and low-cost learning options before expensive programs
- Careful review of accreditation, licensing, contracts, repayment terms, and total cost
- Accessibility and disability-support guidance
- Ethical, private, and responsible use of AI
- Verification through current official sources
- Plain language and practical next steps

## Important Notice

The guides provide education and general career-planning information. They are not legal, tax, medical, immigration, financial, licensing, or individualized professional advice.

Requirements, wages, employment outlook, credentials, costs, and benefits vary by country, state, locality, employer, and time. Verify important decisions through current official sources and qualified professionals.
