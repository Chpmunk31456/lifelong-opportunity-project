# Contributing

Constructive corrections and accessibility improvements are welcome.

## Good Contributions

- Factual corrections supported by authoritative sources
- Broken-link reports
- Accessibility corrections
- Clearer plain-language explanations
- Translation and localization corrections
- Updated licensing, credential, wage, outlook, or education information
- Safer ethical-AI guidance

## Report an Issue

Include:

- Guide number and title
- Language
- Version and file type
- Page or section
- Description of the problem
- Proposed correction
- Supporting official source and access date, when applicable

Do not submit private, confidential, proprietary, or copyrighted material without permission.

## Translation Requirements

Machine-assisted translation may support a draft, but it does not replace human review. A translated edition should identify:

- Source edition and version
- Target language and locale
- Human reviewer or review status
- Date of factual and linguistic review

Do not label a translation as reviewed when a proficient human reviewer has not completed that review.

## Pull Requests

1. Keep unrelated changes separate.
2. Explain what changed and why.
3. Cite current official sources for factual changes.
4. Preserve the project’s warnings against guarantees and predatory programs.
5. Review [ACCESSIBILITY.md](./ACCESSIBILITY.md).
6. Confirm that relative links and filenames work.
7. Update the revision log when a published guide changes materially.

By contributing, you agree that accepted contributions may be distributed under the project’s CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license.
