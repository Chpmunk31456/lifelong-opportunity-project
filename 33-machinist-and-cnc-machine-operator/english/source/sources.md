# Source Review Summary - Machinist and CNC Machine Operator v1.0

- Primary occupation source: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, Machinists and Tool and Die Makers, reviewed July 2026.
- May 2024 median pay used as a national starting point: $56,150 for machinists; $57,700 for the combined group.
- Outlook: Overall group decline of 2% from 2024 to 2034; about 34,200 openings each year.
- Apprenticeship, education, tax, accessibility, labor, and safety statements require current official and jurisdiction-specific verification.
- Next scheduled review: July 2027 or sooner after a material change.
