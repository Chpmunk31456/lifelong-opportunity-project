# Source Review Summary - Construction Laborer and Trade Helper v1.0

- Primary occupation source: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, Construction Laborers and Helpers, reviewed July 2026.
- May 2024 median pay used as a national starting point: $46,050 for the combined group; $46,730 for construction laborers.
- Outlook: 7% growth from 2024 to 2034; about 149,400 openings each year.
- Apprenticeship, education, tax, accessibility, labor, and safety statements require current official and jurisdiction-specific verification.
- Next scheduled review: July 2027 or sooner after a material change.
